Top Recommendation

The Auto Dispense System leads with a weighted score of 4.30, pairing high larva output with strong scalability and long-term reliability.

  • Automation accelerates production consistency.
  • Requires manageable maintenance relative to its benefits.
  • Investment cost aligns with projected performance gains.

Key Observations

  • Maintaining the status quo fails critical production goals despite minimal cost.
  • Manual-Collect performs moderately but suffers from poor scalability.
  • Coop-Integrated Trough excels in affordability yet lags in core performance metrics.
